  • Every file accessed or opened

B. ALIBI VERIFICATION

The precise timestamps in the timeline enable verification or refutation of alibi claims. Each activity is timestamped to the second, establishing a concrete record of the subject's actions on the computer during the period in question.

C. EVIDENCE OF INTENT

Document access patterns, search queries, and application usage can establish intent and premeditation. The timeline shows which files were accessed, when, and for how long — critical evidence in establishing knowledge and purposeful action.
